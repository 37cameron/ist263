What is Block Printing?

Block printing is a form of printmaking where material is carved away from a block to create a stamp. The stamp is dipped into ink so that only the remaining material gets covered. Then the artist stamps the print onto the medium of their choice resulting in a printed design.

I used a carving tool to make a vertabra stamp

Then I rolled ink onto the stamp using a roller

Since HTML is the backbone of webpages, I printed a vertabrae on the back of the T-Shirt
